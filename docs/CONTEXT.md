# Project Context — Fusion Ace Website

## What is Fusion Ace?

Fusion Ace is an **independent real estate consultancy**, not a builder/developer. Its business model is to act as a channel partner to multiple developers and give home buyers/investors an unbiased, side-by-side comparison of projects — rather than a single developer's sales pitch. The service is **free to the buyer**; Fusion Ace is compensated by developers through standard channel partner agreements.

Founder: **Nayeem Munaf Kazi**.

## Target audience

- Home buyers and property investors evaluating multiple residential projects (India / Maharashtra market, specifically Kolhapur-area based on the GST address).
- People wary of aggressive, one-sided developer sales pitches, who want an advocate "on their side of the table."
- Both first-time buyers (need hand-holding, education) and more experienced investors (want efficient, curated shortlists).

## Tone & brand voice

- **Trustworthy, independent, transparent** — explicitly positions itself as *not* a builder, and emphasizes it filters out developers with poor track records.
- **Not pushy / no hard-sell** — consultative, advisory tone. Copy reads like a founder speaking directly and honestly ("Hi, I'm Nayeem Munaf Kazi...").
- **Plain-spoken, confident, slightly informal-professional** — short punchy headline sentences, no corporate jargon.
- Design should reinforce this: calm, editorial, premium — not a flashy/aggressive sales site, not a generic blue SaaS template either.
